# Generative AI & Foundation Models Research Notebook

A centralized digital repository documenting theoretical foundations, mathematical derivations, and hands-on implementations of advanced deep learning architectures and generative AI paradigms.

The goal of this repository is to bridge the gap between reading foundational research papers and understanding their underlying mechanics through rigorous notation and clean code.

---

## 🔬 Core Focus Areas

* **Transformers & Attention:** Self-attention mechanics, cross-attention, scalable LLM architectures, and efficient attention variants.
* **Energy-Based & Self-Supervised Learning:** Non-contrastive representation learning, focusing on Yann LeCun's Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture (**JEPA**).
* **Generative Frameworks:** Variational Autoencoders (**VAEs** & ELBO optimization), Generative Adversarial Networks (**GANs**), and latent space topologies.
* **Foundation Models (FMs):** Emerging paradigms in multi-modal systems, self-supervised pre-training, and scaling laws.
