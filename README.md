# 🧠 Generative AI & Foundation Models Research Notebook

A comprehensive knowledge base documenting the theoretical foundations, mathematical principles, architectural designs, and practical implementations of modern Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Generative AI, and Foundation Models.

This repository serves as my personal research notebook for studying how modern AI systems work—from first principles to state-of-the-art architectures. Rather than simply summarizing research papers, the goal is to **understand why these models work, how they are designed, and how they can be implemented from scratch.**

Each topic combines:

* 📖 Intuitive explanations
* 📐 Mathematical derivations
* 🧩 Architectural breakdowns
* 📄 Insights from foundational research papers
* 💻 PyTorch implementations and experiments
* 📊 Visualizations and diagrams where appropriate

The long-term vision is to build a structured learning resource covering the evolution of AI systems—from classical neural networks to future world models and autonomous AI agents.

---

# 🎯 Learning Objectives

This repository aims to bridge the gap between:

```text
Research Papers
        ↓
Mathematical Understanding
        ↓
Architectural Intuition
        ↓
Implementation from Scratch
        ↓
Real-World Applications
```

Rather than treating AI models as black boxes, each architecture is studied from the ground up, focusing on the reasoning behind every design decision.
